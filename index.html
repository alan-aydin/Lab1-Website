Fumble is a platform where students can help each other with school-related tasks. You post what you need help with, others can respond, and you choose the person who fits best.

Students can exchange study support, feedback on school projects, brainstorming help, explanations of difficult topics, and guidance for creative or technical assignments.

Fumble focuses on learning support, not on doing work for someone. Users must follow school guidelines, and helpers provide explanations, tips, and feedback.
